What We Do

Our recycling process ensures maximum sustainability with zero compromise.

1. Collection

Waste is picked up from homes, industries & commercial spaces.

2. Sorting

Labels removed, caps separated & PET categorized.

3. Grinding

Bottles are shredded into uniform, high-quality flakes.

4. Washing

Hot water wash + detergent cleaning for purity.

5. Drying

Flakes are fully dried to remove moisture, ensuring quality.

6. Ready for Use

Final PET flakes supplied to industries for new product creation.
