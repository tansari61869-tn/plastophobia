What We Do

We collect, process, and recycle PET-based waste materials responsibly. Our factory is tuned for sustainability.

What We Accept

PET Water Bottles

All sizes – 250ml to 2L. Clean or crushed bottles accepted. The core of our recycling intake.

Juice & Soda Bottles

PET bottles from FMCG brands, soft drinks & juices. Must be empty of liquid.

Oil Bottles

Used PET oil bottles are accepted after proper draining and basic cleaning process.

Food Containers

Transparent PET containers used in food packaging. Must be free of food residue.

PET Sheets & Straps

Industrial PET waste suitable for grinding and reuse in various applications.

Our Process

1. Collection

Waste is picked up from homes, industries & commercial spaces.

2. Sorting

Labels removed, caps separated & PET categorized meticulously.

3. Grinding

Bottles are shredded into uniform, high-quality plastic flakes.

4. Ready for Use

Cleaned flakes supplied to industries for new product creation.
