About Plastophobia

From plastic waste to purposeful materials – this is our story.

Our Mission

To radically reduce plastic waste by converting it into durable, high-quality products that power construction, landscaping, and everyday life.

We see value where others see waste. Our facility is equipped to handle tonnes of PET bottles daily, ensuring they don't end up in landfills or oceans.

15 Tonnes of PET, 22.5 Tonnes of CO₂ Saved

Same eco-magic, just scaled up. When we recycle 15 tonnes of PET bottles, the climate impact is massive:

  • Base logic: 3 tonnes PET → 4.5 tonnes CO₂ saved.
  • That’s 1.5 tonnes CO₂ saved for every tonne of PET we recycle.
  • So 15 tonnes PET → 22.5 tonnes of CO₂ kept out of the air.
