About Plastophobia

From plastic waste to purposeful materials – this is our story.

Our Mission

To radically reduce plastic waste by converting it into durable, high–quality products that power construction, landscaping and everyday life.

What We Do

We collect and process post–consumer and industrial plastic waste, grind it in our in–house facility and prepare it for moulding into various applications. Every kilogram we handle is one step away from landfills and oceans.

Core Strengths

  • In–house grinding & processing
  • Consistent flake quality
  • Flexible for future product lines
